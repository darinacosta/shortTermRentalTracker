Data Disclaimer

The short term rental listings featured on the Nola Short Term Rental Report are collected using the Zilyo API. After these listings are compiled, we search through the individual listings in this compiled list in order to collect additional data on the listings themselves and on user behavior.

Although the data featured in the report hav been produced and processed from information that is publicly advertised on vacation rental websites, no warranty is made regarding accuracy, adequacy, completeness, legality, reliability or usefulness of any information. There is no gauranteevas to the spatial accuracy of individual vacation rental listings featured on the map. These spatial points are not associated with residential addresses, and are only intended to communicate the general area of the listing.
