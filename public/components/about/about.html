About

The Nola Short Term Rental Report is a living document. The content and data is likely to improve as research and analysis continue. The listings featured on the map are collected using the Zilyo API. After these listings are compiled, the individual listings in this compiled list are analyzed in order to collect additional data on the listings themselves and on user behavior. The data is updated every three days. For a more detailed analysis of data related specifically to Airbnb both in New Orleans and in many other cities, please visit Inside Airbnb.

GET THE DATA

Our data is available for download as comma-seperated text files. If you use our data, please consider donating to our volunteer-run project in order to help keep our servers running.

Download the delimited text files here: Airbnb | HomeAway

DISCLAIMER

The coordinates of the data points featured on the map are provided by the short term rental listing websites, and the spatial accuracy is not precise. They are not associated with specific addresses, and are only intended to communicate the general area of the listing. Although both the spatial and non-spatial data featured across the site have been produced and processed from information that is publicly advertised on vacation rental websites, no warranty is made regarding accuracy, adequacy, completeness, legality, reliability or usefulness of the data.
